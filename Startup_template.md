# BlinkBudget

Tagline: Understand your money mood

## Concept

Emotional spending tracker

## Full Context

BlinkBudget analyzes transaction data and optional wearable mood signals to detect patterns like stress spending or celebratory splurges. Instead of rigid budgets, it provides behavioral insight and gentle nudges before impulse purchases happen.

## Target Users

Young porfessionals and freelancers

# Core Problem

Budgeting apps ignore emotional triggers

# Your Challenge

Make finance feel human and insightful
